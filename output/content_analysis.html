Methodology

  • Source: 2,162 posts and 24,871 comments scraped from r/Peptides (March 2026).
  • Extraction: Each post + its comments sent to Claude Haiku 3.5 via Anthropic Batch API. The LLM extracts structured reports: compounds, doses, routes, benefits, side effects (with severity 1–5), sentiment, and confidence tier.
  • Normalization: Raw compound names cleaned and mapped to a curated registry of 265+ compounds.
  • Aggregation: Individual reports rolled up into compound profiles. Side effect categories merged where appropriate (e.g. blood_pressure + heart_rate → cardiac). Percentages are against total reports for that compound.
  • Confidence tiers: T3 = bloodwork/labs, T4 = detailed protocol, T5 = anecdotal mention.
  • Limitations: Self-reported community data, not clinical trials. Subject to selection bias, recall bias, and LLM extraction error.
